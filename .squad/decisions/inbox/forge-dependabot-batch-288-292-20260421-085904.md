## Operational note

Branch protection requires BOTH `Analyze (actions)` AND `rubberduck-gate` (strict=true), not just `Analyze (actions)` as the runbook stated. With strict=true, every merge invalidates the remaining PRs in a batch and forces a `gh pr update-branch` + ~90s CI wait per subsequent merge. Recommend running batches sequentially, not in parallel.
